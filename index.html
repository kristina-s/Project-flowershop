Our flower-shop and procution process

Previous Next

Bonsai flower-shop is a combination of both branches of a family owned business, with our own manufacturing process, as well as importing flowers from various countries. The manufacturing process is located is Skopje on a 4000m2 area and a greenhouse. At the same time we have imported flowers from Netherlands, Equador etc. that we distributed through flower and gift shops in the capital. We offer many years of floral experience and we have a reputation for being a good partner in floral and gift industry. We have spent many years building trusted and reliable relationships with the best florist and gift shops across our country. This means that your flower arrangements include only the finest quality flowers, that are carefully grown on conditions unique for each type of flower.
